## Content Categories

### 1. Analysis (`/analysis`)

Deep tactical breakdowns, system explanations, trend analysis.

**Analysis Subcategories:**

- Tactical Breakdowns
- System Analysis
- Coaching Decisions
- Set Piece Strategy
- Data Insights

**Analysis Content Types:**

- Match tactical analysis (2000-3000 words)
- Formation deep-dives (1500-2500 words)
- Pressing system explainers (1500-2000 words)
- Season-long tactical trends (2500-4000 words)

---

### 2. News (`/news`)

Match reports, transfer updates, injury news, squad developments.

**News Subcategories:**

- Match Reports
- Transfer News
- Squad Updates
- Injury Reports
- Manager News

**News Content Types:**

- Match reports with analysis (1000-1500 words)
- Transfer analysis (800-1200 words)
- Quick news updates (300-500 words)
- Press conference breakdowns (600-1000 words)

---

### 3. Features (`/features`)

Player profiles, long-form stories, cultural pieces, historical deep-dives.

**Features Subcategories:**

- Player Profiles
- Manager Spotlights
- Club Features
- Football Culture
- Historical Analysis

**Features Content Types:**

- Player deep-dive profiles (2500-4000 words)
- Manager tactical philosophy pieces (2000-3000 words)
- Club identity and culture (2000-3500 words)
- Football history features (1500-3000 words)

---

### 4. Match Center (`/match-center`)

Fixtures, results, league tables, head-to-head stats.

**Match Center Sections:**

- Upcoming Fixtures
- Recent Results
- League Tables (Premier League, Champions League, etc.)
- Head-to-Head Records

**Match Center Content:**

- Live match tracker (automated)
- Post-match stats and highlights
- Fixture previews (500-800 words)

---

### 5. Tactical Guides (`/guides`)

Evergreen educational content, system explainers, role definitions.

**Tactical Guides Subcategories:**

- Formation Guides (4-3-3, 3-4-3, 4-2-3-1, etc.)
- Positional Roles (False 9, Inverted Wingback, etc.)
- Tactical Concepts (Pressing, Counter-Pressing, Build-Up Play)
- Football Terminology

**Tactical Guides Content Types:**

- Complete formation guides (2000-3000 words each)
- Position explainers (1200-1800 words)
- Tactical concept deep-dives (1500-2500 words)
- Beginner-friendly explainers (800-1500 words)

---

### 6. Opinion (`/opinion`)

Hot takes, predictions, controversial perspectives, debates.

**Opinion Content Types:**

- Match reaction opinions (800-1200 words)
- Season predictions (1500-2000 words)
- Transfer window takes (1000-1500 words)
- Debate pieces (1200-2000 words)
- Manager/player criticism or praise (1000-1800 words)

---

### 7. Player Profiles (`/players`)

Dedicated player pages with stats, analysis, and updates.

**Player Profile Structure:**

- Player overview
- Role and tactical fit
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Career trajectory
- Latest news and updates

## Internal Linking Strategy

### From Match Reports → Analysis

Link to tactical breakdown of the same match.

### From Player Profiles → Features

Link to long-form player deep-dives.

### From News → Guides

Link to relevant tactical explainers.
*Example: Transfer news about a false nine → Link to "What is a False 9?" guide.*

### From Analysis → Opinion

Link to related hot takes or debate pieces.

### From Guides → Analysis

"See this concept in action" → Link to recent match analysis using that system.

### Contextual Linking Rules

Every article should have 3-5 internal links to related content:

- "Read our full tactical breakdown of X"
- "See our player profile on Y"
- "Learn more about this formation in our guide"
- "We predicted this in our season preview"
